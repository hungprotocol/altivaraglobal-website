Data Protection & Processing

Altivara approaches information handling with appropriate control, proportionality, and business discipline.

Where Altivara processes information in connection with inquiries, communications, or engagement discussions, we aim to do so in a manner that is appropriate to the nature of the information, business purpose, and applicable obligations.

We seek to limit access to information to authorized personnel, advisers, and service providers with a legitimate operational need. We also aim to retain information only for as long as reasonably required for business, legal, or compliance purposes.

If a formal client engagement requires more detailed terms related to data handling, confidentiality, processing scope, or security obligations, those terms would ordinarily be addressed in the relevant contract documentation.

For data protection inquiries, contact contact@altivaraglobal.com.
